Real Farm, Real Story

Step into Our Farm

Come see where your food comes from. We have nothing to hide. Only fresh grass, clean air, and happy animals.

The Green Land

This is where it all begins. Our farm is spread across many acres of green land in North India. We grow our own food for the animals here.

Image Description: Above, you can see our clean farm sheds and green fields. It is a quiet place far away from city pollution, where the air is fresh and the soil is healthy.

Our Beloved Gir Cows

We take care of the pure Gir breed. These cows are known for their health and the special A2 protein in their milk.

Image Description: Here are our Gir cows. Notice their unique humps and ears. They spend their day walking around the farm and eating fresh organic grass. We never use any harmful injections on them.

The Strong Murrah Buffaloes

For those who love thick and creamy milk, we have the best Murrah buffaloes. Their milk is best for making Ghee and Paneer at home.

Image Description: These black buffaloes are the pride of our farm. They are kept in very clean areas with cool water. They are strong, healthy, and give milk that is full of natural cream.

Love and Care

Machines help us keep things clean, but the heart of our farm is our people. Our farmers know every animal by their name.

Image Description: You can see one of our senior farmers caring for a cow. We believe that a cow that feels loved gives the healthiest milk for your children. We follow a very hygienic process from start to finish.

Why we are different?

Pure Food

Our animals eat only natural grass and grains. No chemicals ever.

Fast Delivery

From milking to your door in less than 5 hours. Always cold.

No Mixing

We don't mix water or powder. You get exactly what the cow gives.
