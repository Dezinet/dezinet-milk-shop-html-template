Privacy Policy.

Last Updated: April 2026 | Version: 1.0

Your Privacy Matters to Us

At Fresh Milk Shop, we respect your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

1. What We Collect

We only collect the information we need to deliver your milk and dairy products:

  • Your Name: To know who to deliver to.
  • Your Address: To deliver to the right place.
  • Your Phone Number: To call you if there is a problem with the delivery.
  • Your Email: To send you receipts and order updates.

2. How We Use Informatiom

We use your information only for these things:

  • To manage your account and subscriptions.
  • To deliver your orders.
  • To show you products you might like.
  • To talk to you about your orders.

3. We Don't Sell Your Data

We will never sell your name, address, or phone number to anyone else. We only share it with our delivery team so they can find your home.

4. Security

We use modern security systems to keep your information safe. We don't save your credit card or bank details on our computers. They are handled by safe payment partners like Razorpay or Paytm.

5. Your Rights

You can see all your information in your account settings. You can also ask us to delete your account at any time by emailing us.

6. Cookies

Our website uses small files called cookies to remember what you put in your cart and keep you logged in. These are standard and safe to use.

7. Questions

If you want to know more about how we keep your data safe, please email us at hi@freshmilkshop.in.
